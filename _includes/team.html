Hi! My name is Bumjun Park. I am from Seongnam, South Korea, and I am a doctoral student in Biostatistics at the University of Washington. I am a graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a degree in Statistics and certificates in Mathematics and Economic Analytics. I am a native speaker of English and Korean, with competencies in French and Spanish. My academic interests include spatial and environmental statistics, functional data analysis, and phylogenetic modeling. I work with Professor Eardi Lila, Department of Biostatistics, on a team led by Professor Mahmud Mossa-Basha, Department of Radiology, to develop quantitative models for reclassifying Embolic Strokes of Undetermined Sources (ESUS) using cerebral vessel wall imaging data.
